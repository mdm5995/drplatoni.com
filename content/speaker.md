## Army Retirement Speech

*July 19, 2014*

Recently [Special Speeches](https://www.specialspeeches.com/military-retirement-speeches.html) asked to include my retirement speech on their website. I am honored to be included with the best, right under Colin Powell!

Retirement . . . such an ugly word in my vocabulary. Out to pasture. Gone to bed. Removed from the work force. Taken out of circulation. Sending the old battleship out to the mothball fleet. None of these terms sound the least bit appealing.
